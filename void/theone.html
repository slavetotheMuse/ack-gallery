The 10K PFP

a.c.k. — 2026

A single ERC-721 whose image is all 10,000 possible Argonauts collapsed into one. 37 traits across 7 layers cycle forever via pure SVG/SMIL keyframes — no HTML, no JavaScript, no IPFS. The entire animation lives inside the contract as bytecode. What renders in your wallet is exactly what renders here.

The collector controls the tempo. A setSpeed(1..4) call toggles between four pre-rendered speed slots, callable by the artist or the current holder. Every bid placed on the initial auction is recorded as a witness — the token's description updates live and crystallizes forever with the winning bid at settlement.

Artist Statement

On the Collapse

I've always been fascinated by on chain generative art. There's no competing with Cryptopunks, and there's no reason to make another 10K PFP. The format has been done a million times. I don't want to build something that becomes a Discord floor price thread.

Argonauts were born a year ago as an April Fools fake mint. I never stopped thinking about them. I revisited them often. I even built out a free claim for ACK holders of all 10,000, fully working, ready to go, and it still didn't feel right.

What feels right is this: the entire smart contract as a generative on chain 1 of 1. You own the entity called Argonauts. All of them, at once. Pure SVG, no HTML, no IPFS, no off chain anything. The artwork lives inside the contract as bytecode. The generator is still there, runnable forever, so anyone can see the full possibility space the piece was drawn from.

I don't know if it's been done before. Many things have. For me, this feels right. I had never made a fully on chain artwork on Ethereum before this. Argonauts deserve to exist, as one entity, and now they do.

a.c.k., 2026
